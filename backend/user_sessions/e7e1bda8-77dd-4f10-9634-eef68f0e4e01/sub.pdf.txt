Sub-38 Min 10K Leg Workout

Duration: 50-60 minutes | Thursday + Sunday

Phase 1: Explosive Power (15 min)

A1. Depth Jumps: 4 x 5 (2-3 min rest)

A2. Single-Leg Bounds: 3 x 6 each leg (90s rest)
B1. Reactive Box Jumps: 4 x 4 (2 min rest)

B2. Jump Lunges: 3 x 12 total (90s rest)

Phase 2: Strength Endurance (20 min)

[Page 3 OCR]
C1. Bulgarian Split Squats: 4 x 12 each leg (45s rest)
C2. Single-Leg RDL: 4 x 10 each leg (45s rest)

D1. Single-Leg Calf Raises: 4 x 20 each leg (30s rest)
D2. Lateral Bounds: 3 x 10 each direction (60s rest)

Phase 3: Power Endurance (12 min)

E1. Stadium Steps: 5 x 20 steps (walk down recovery)
E2. Single-Leg Hops: 4 x 20 each leg (30s rest)
E3. Wall Sit: 3 x 45-60 seconds (45s rest)

Phase 4: Metabolic Finisher (8 min)
Circuit (3 rounds, minimal rest):

¢ Squat jumps x 15

¢ Mountain climbers x 20

© Pogo jumps x 15

e High knees x 20

Notes: Sunday sessions reduce plyometric volume by 30%. Week 4 & 6: reduce all volumes by 25%.

Race Strategy for Sub-38

Start (0-2km): 3:52-3:55/km - conservative start
Middle (2-7km): 3:46-3:50/km - settle into race pace
Finish (7-10km): 3:42-3:48/km - empty the tank
